Biking

July 10, 2016 by Dmitry

Hi, my name is Dima, I'm from Dnepropetrovsk and in this post i decided to tell you how i spend my free time. One of the ways is cycling! I really like it. Also it is very good for health.

Its my old retro bike. It was released in 1990 and its older than me. Maybe it looks not so good but dont believe what you see. It is very powerful beast!
You can overcome a long distance on a bike. While cycling you can see many interesting things and places.
There are a lot of industrial landscapes with old plants and factories in Dnepropetrovsk.
In addition bicycle is environmentally friendly form of transport. Its very important in our polluted industrial city.
During the weekend roads are almost empty.
The day is over and its says goodbye to us with a beautiful sunset :)
It was a long trip, so its time to have a rest.
I was tired but full of impressions. if you have never been in long cycling trip you have to try!
